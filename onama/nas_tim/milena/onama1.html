Biography

Jovana Milovanovic joined the Marketing group in the Management department at Birkbeck in 2011. She holds a BA from Dublin City University, a PGCE from the University of London, and a PhD from the University of Edinburgh. Her research interest focuses on consumer culture theory, interpretive consumer research and gender. She is also interested in practical and entrepreneurial implications of gender and consumer research.
