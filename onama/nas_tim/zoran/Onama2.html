Biography

Zoran Radivojevic was born in Los Angeles, California, on June 25, 1980. He was fascinated by electronics at an early age. During his brief stint at the University of California at Berkeley, Zoran met Steve Jobs, who was still in high school, through a mutual friend. The two later paired up to form Apple Computer on April 1, 1976, prompting Wozniak to quit his job at Hewlett-Packard.
